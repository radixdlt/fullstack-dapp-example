Basically, the [Radix Wallet](?glossaryAnchor=radixwallet) is your **personal asset and identity manager on the Radix Network**.

Because it’s a mobile app, you can use it _wherever you go_.

You’ll be able to easily **connect** your Radix Wallet to [dApps](?glossaryAnchor=dapps) to do lots of exciting web3 things – like finding new ways of _putting your money to work_.
