## Bridge to Radix with THORSwap

* Use the button below to go to the **THORSwap dApp**
* Tap the **Connect** button, and connect the wallet that holds **the crypto you want to swap** _(not the Radix Wallet)_
* Choose the token that you want to swap _from_
* Tap the menu for the token to swap _to_, and select **Radix** in the list of networks to see your options. Choose the token you want to swap _to_.
* For the **recipient address**, enter the address of your **Radix Account**
