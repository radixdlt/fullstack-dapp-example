## RadMorphs

Each RadMorph is created from a unique combination of your choice of **2** RadGems and **1** Morph Energy Card…

**POOF**, they are replaced by a beautiful – and possibly rare – RadMorph.

**2 RadGems + 1 Energy Card**
**→ 1 RadMorph**
