## Creating RadMorphs

Basically, if you combine **2** RadGems of your choice with **1** Morph Energy Card… **POOF**, they are replaced by a beautiful – and possibly rare – RadMorph.

**2 RadGems + 1 Energy Card**
**→ 1 RadMorph**
